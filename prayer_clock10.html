🌙 Fasting Six Days of Shawwāl

‏مَنْ صَامَ رَمَضَانَ ثُمَّ أَتْبَعَهُ سِتًّا مِنْ شَوَّالٍ كَانَ كَصِيَامِ الدَّهْرِ‏

  • The Prophet ﷺ said:
  • “Whoever fasts Ramadan and then follows it with six days of Shawwāl, it is as if he fasted for a lifetime.”

📖 Sahih Muslim # 1164

🕌 Calculation & Explanation

Quran:

مَن جَآءَ بِٱلْحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُۥ عَشْرُ أَمْثَالِهَا ۖ وَمَن جَآءَ بِٱلسَّيِّئَةِ فَلَا يُجْزَىٰٓ إِلَّا مِثْلَهَا وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ

  • Whoever comes with a good deed will be rewarded tenfold.
  • But whoever comes with a bad deed will be punished for only one.
  • None will be wronged.

📖 Surah Al An'am - Verse # 160

🌙 Total Days in Islamic (Hijri) calendar

The Islamic (Hijri) calendar is a lunar calendar, and its total number of days depends on the year:

354 days in a normal year & 355 days in a leap year

  • Ramadan = 30 days × 10 reward = 300 days
  • 6 days of Shawwāl × 10 reward = 60 days
  • Total = 360 days (≈ a full year)
